Hannah Takeda

Digital Media Art Student

Hannah Takeda is a Japanese-American multimedia artist based in the Bay Area, currently studying at San Jose State University. She works across a range of mediums including digital and traditional illustration, photography, and video editing—often using tools like Procreate, Adobe Premiere, and Adobe Lightroom. Inspired by her family, nature, and the aesthetics of anime, Hannah’s work reflects her evolving artistic voice and deep passion for visual storytelling. While she has not yet committed to a singular thematic focus, Hannah views her art as an open-ended journey of exploration and growth. Much of her digital work features both original and fan-made characters influenced by anime and video games, blending imaginative storytelling with her desire to develop technical skills and creative expression. She embraces each new project as an opportunity to experiment with different materials and techniques, continually pushing the boundaries of her artistic comfort zone.
